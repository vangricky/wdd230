Sacramento Chamber of Commerce

The Sacramento Chamber of Commerce, a beacon of economic growth and community empowerment, stands as the heart and soul of the bustling Californian capital. Established in 1855, this dynamic organization has been a driving force behind Sacramento's remarkable evolution. With a membership that includes a diverse array of businesses, from tech startups to family-owned restaurants, the Chamber fosters collaboration and innovation. Through its tireless advocacy efforts, the Chamber has played a pivotal role in shaping policies that encourage entrepreneurship and sustainability in the region. The Chamber's annual "Sacramento Showcase" event has become a must-attend gathering, where local businesses can showcase their innovations, connect with investors, and chart a path towards success in the vibrant Sacramento market.
