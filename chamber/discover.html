Demographic

Sacramento, California boasts a diverse population with a rich blend of cultures and backgrounds. Its demographics reflect a vibrant and dynamic community.

Statistics

Sacramento, California has a growing population, with over half a million residents as of my last update in 2021. The city is also known for its sunny weather, with an average of 265 days of sunshine annually.

Events

Sacramento, California hosts a wide range of events throughout the year, from the lively Sacramento Music Festival to the prestigious California State Fair. The city's calendar is always filled with exciting opportunities for residents and visitors alike.
